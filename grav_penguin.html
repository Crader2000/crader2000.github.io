Gravity Penguin

Gravity Penguin is a 3D platformer with an emphasis on fast movement and levels that lend themselves to that speed. Inspired by the Sonic the Hedgehog games.

First time using widget blueprints. This project was done during my junior year of college, and it was my first exposure to Unreal engine. In contrast to B.L.A.S.T., I worked all in blueprints for this project. It addition to this being my first time using widgets, I also animated the health bar. Below, you can see the lose screen. You lose the game when your health is reduce to 0, or you fall off of the map.
